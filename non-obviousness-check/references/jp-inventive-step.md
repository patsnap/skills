# JP Inventive Step Quick Guide

Use this guide for Japanese inventive-step conclusions.

## Practical Flow

1. Recognize the cited invention.
2. Identify differences against the claimed invention.
3. Evaluate motivation, compatibility, and impeding factors.
4. Check advantageous or unexpected effects when supported by evidence.

## Practical Rules

- Keep the difference analysis specific and feature-based.
- Evaluate whether the art gives a reason to bridge each difference.
- Record impeding factors such as contrary teaching, incompatibility, or loss of function.
- Unexpected effects matter more when tied tightly to the actual differences.

## Common Traps

- vague difference statements
- assuming the same field automatically creates motivation
- missing impeding factors
- moving technical-effect arguments into novelty by mistake

## Output Notes

State:

- cited invention baseline
- key differences
- motivation and impeding factors
- overall JP inventive-step view
