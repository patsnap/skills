# Domain Playbooks

Use the relevant section before final tiering and route-differentiation claims.

## Semiconductor And Hardware

- Distinguish integrated device makers, foundries, fabless firms, EDA/IP firms,
  equipment vendors, and packaging players.
- Prioritize patent family breadth, process node claims, shipment evidence,
  standards position, and ecosystem dependencies.
- Do not merge all hardware players into one matrix without role labeling.

## Biopharma And Medtech

- Distinguish platform companies, asset companies, diagnostics, device makers,
  contract enablers, and academic feeders.
- Prioritize pipeline stage, target coverage, regulatory signals, and clinical or
  translational evidence.
- Do not rank early science players as commercial leaders without downstream proof.

## AI And Software

- Distinguish model builders, infrastructure vendors, application companies,
  platform providers, and open-source ecosystem leaders.
- Prioritize benchmarks, deployment, platform adoption, OSS gravity, and partner traction.
- Do not let social visibility outrank technical and production evidence.

## New Energy And Materials

- Distinguish material innovators, process suppliers, cell/system integrators,
  equipment makers, and deployment leaders.
- Prioritize scale-up, cost, lifetime/safety, supply-chain leverage, and standards signals.
- Do not treat lab results alone as competitive leadership.

## Industrial And General Manufacturing

- Distinguish core process owners, system integrators, subsystem suppliers, and
  certification/standards leaders.
- Prioritize installed base, process know-how, field deployment, and bottleneck position.
- Do not overstate fragmented sectors as if they were winner-take-all arenas.
