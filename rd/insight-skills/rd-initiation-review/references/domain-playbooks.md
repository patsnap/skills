# Domain Playbooks

Use the relevant section when reviewing concrete projects in domain-heavy areas.

## Semiconductor And Hardware

- Check whether the proposal is claiming device physics, process engineering,
  packaging/integration, equipment capability, or design-tool advantage.
- Prioritize manufacturability, integration risk, and supply-chain dependencies.
- Flag missing prototype, yield, or process-window evidence early.

## Biopharma And Medtech

- Check whether the proposal is claiming mechanism novelty, target novelty,
  platform novelty, diagnostic utility, or device usability.
- Prioritize translational evidence, regulatory path, trial readiness, and patient/data access.
- Flag missing validation cohorts, safety path, or regulatory strategy early.

## AI And Software

- Check whether the proposal is claiming algorithmic novelty, system novelty,
  data advantage, deployment efficiency, or workflow integration.
- Prioritize benchmark integrity, data availability, reproducibility, and deployment readiness.
- Flag missing evaluation design, baseline choice, or production constraints early.

## New Energy And Materials

- Check whether the proposal is claiming composition novelty, process novelty,
  architecture novelty, or system integration novelty.
- Prioritize scale-up, lifetime, safety, cost path, and supply-chain viability.
- Flag missing engineering validation beyond lab performance early.

## Industrial And General Manufacturing

- Check whether the proposal is claiming process control, equipment, automation,
  quality uplift, or systems integration improvements.
- Prioritize field conditions, standards/certification path, and integration with existing operations.
- Flag missing pilot data, acceptance criteria, or deployment constraints early.
