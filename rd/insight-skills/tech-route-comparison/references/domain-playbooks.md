# Domain Playbooks

Use the relevant section before ranking routes.

## Semiconductor And Hardware

- Compare routes at a consistent layer: architecture, process, packaging, or system.
- Add integration risk, manufacturing readiness, supply-chain dependence, and thermal/power constraints.
- Do not rank routes only on patent or paper activity.

## Biopharma And Medtech

- Compare routes at the same therapeutic, diagnostic, or device layer.
- Add translational feasibility, regulatory path, validation burden, and evidence quality.
- Do not rank routes only on conceptual novelty.

## AI And Software

- Compare routes at the same layer: model family, systems stack, or application architecture.
- Add data dependency, reproducibility, deployment efficiency, and benchmark relevance.
- Do not rank routes only on leaderboard or paper visibility.

## New Energy And Materials

- Compare routes at the same layer: chemistry, component, pack, or system.
- Add manufacturability, cost path, lifetime/safety, and supply-chain readiness.
- Do not rank routes only on lab-scale performance.

## Industrial And General Manufacturing

- Compare routes at the same layer: process method, equipment architecture, or plant/system integration.
- Add integration burden, certification path, maintenance complexity, and field reliability.
- Do not rank routes only on conceptual elegance or pilot anecdotes.
