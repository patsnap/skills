# Scenario Templates and Scoring Weights

Select one scenario before ranking options.

## 1. Enterprise Engineering Template

### Typical use
Product development, manufacturing improvement, reliability/cost optimization.

### Required inputs
- business objective and KPI
- current baseline and pain point
- implementation deadline
- budget and resource limits
- safety/compliance constraints

### Evaluation focus
- delivery risk
- deployment complexity
- ROI and maintainability

### Scoring weights (total 100)
- Impact: 30
- Feasibility: 30
- CostEfficiency: 20
- RiskControl: 15
- Novelty: 5

## 2. Research Project Template

### Typical use
Lab research, grant proposal, mechanism discovery, method innovation.

### Required inputs
- research question and novelty target
- hypothesis and current evidence
- experimental constraints
- available instruments/data
- publication or project timeline

### Evaluation focus
- novelty and knowledge contribution
- verifiability and reproducibility
- uncertainty management

### Scoring weights (total 100)
- Impact: 25
- Feasibility: 15
- CostEfficiency: 10
- RiskControl: 20
- Novelty: 30

## 3. Teaching/Learning Project Template

### Typical use
Course projects, capstone design, guided innovation training.

### Required inputs
- learning objective
- student skill level and available time
- tool/material availability
- deliverable format and rubric

### Evaluation focus
- learnability
- implementation practicality
- educational value

### Scoring weights (total 100)
- Impact: 25
- Feasibility: 25
- CostEfficiency: 20
- RiskControl: 15
- Novelty: 15

## 4. Score Formula
For each option:
- dimension score: 1-5
- weighted score = sum(dimension score x weight)
- rank by weighted score (high to low)

## 5. Confidence Label
Attach confidence label per option:
- High: strong evidence + clear feasibility path
- Medium: partial evidence or moderate assumptions
- Low: limited evidence or high integration uncertainty
