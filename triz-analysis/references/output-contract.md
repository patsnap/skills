# Output Contract (TRIZ Analysis Report v2)

Use this structure in final responses.

## 1. Problem and Objective
- Problem statement
- Target metric(s)
- Baseline
- Scope and constraints

## 2. Contradiction Analysis
- Technical contradiction(s)
- Physical contradiction(s) if any
- Key bottleneck summary

## 3. Evidence Pack (Mandatory)
At least 3 entries:

| ID | Title | Type | Year | URL | Why relevant | Mapped option |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| E1 | ... | patent/paper | ... | ... | ... | Conservative/Balanced/Breakthrough |

## 4. Three Options (Mandatory)

### Option A: Conservative
- Core idea
- Expected gains
- Required resources
- Risks and mitigations
- Evidence links

### Option B: Balanced
- Core idea
- Expected gains
- Required resources
- Risks and mitigations
- Evidence links

### Option C: Breakthrough
- Core idea
- Expected gains
- Required resources
- Risks and mitigations
- Evidence links

## 5. Quantitative Score Table (Mandatory)

| Option | Impact | Feasibility | CostEfficiency | RiskControl | Novelty | Weighted total | Confidence |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| A | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | ... | High/Medium/Low |
| B | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | ... | High/Medium/Low |
| C | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | 1-5 | ... | High/Medium/Low |

## 6. Recommendation
- Recommended option
- Why this option now
- Key tradeoff accepted
- Conditions where recommendation should be reconsidered

## 7. Validation Roadmap (Mandatory)
- Minimal viable experiment
- Success/failure thresholds
- Timeline and owners/resources
- Top risks
- Stop-loss criteria
- Next data to collect

## 8. Assumptions and Open Risks
Separate clearly:
- Facts (evidence-backed)
- Inferences (reasoned but uncertain)
- Unknowns requiring follow-up

## 9. Quality Checklist
Before finalizing, verify:
1. At least 3 credible evidence sources are included.
2. Each option has evidence mapping.
3. Ranking is consistent with scenario weights.
4. Recommendation includes explicit tradeoff.
5. Validation roadmap is actionable.
